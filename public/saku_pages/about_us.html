About Us

Saku Global Tech Tabs Pvt Ltd is a future-focused education and technology consulting organization committed to bridging the gap between academia, industry, and emerging technologies. We partner with universities, educational institutions, and learners to build capabilities in Artificial Intelligence, Semiconductors, and Industry 4.0 technologies while fostering globally relevant educational ecosystems.

By combining deep industry insights with academic excellence, we help institutions stay ahead of technological disruption and prepare students for success in a rapidly evolving global workforce.
