A comprehensive programme taking learners from ML fundamentals through cutting-edge AI including NLP, LLMs, Reinforcement Learning, and Responsible AI. Graduates will have the skills to design, build, and deploy real-world AI applications, culminating in a full-pipeline capstone project.

Learning Outcomes

  • Build neural networks, CNNs for vision, RNNs for sequences, and Transformers for NLP
  • Work with tokenisation, word embeddings, GPT, BERT, T5, and prompt engineering
  • Apply RL concepts where agents learn from environments through reward-based systems
  • Implement a complete AI solution from problem definition through to deployment
