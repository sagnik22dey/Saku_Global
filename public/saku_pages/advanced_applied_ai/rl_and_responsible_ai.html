Covers RL principles and applications, combining core RL algorithms with a dedicated module on ethical AI development. Learners create intelligent agents capable of decision-making in dynamic environments using Q-Learning and Deep Q Networks (DQN), and develop competence in responsible AI design.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand RL basics including MDPs, reward structures, and policy-based methods
  • Implement core RL algorithms: Q-learning, policy gradients, and DQN
  • Design AI systems that are fair, transparent, and accountable
  • Apply privacy-preserving AI techniques including differential privacy
  • Implement strategies to mitigate bias in AI systems
