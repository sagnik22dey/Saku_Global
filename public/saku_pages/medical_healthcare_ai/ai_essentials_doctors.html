AI Essentials for Doctors

4 Hours · 100% Video-based · MBBS/MD/DNB Doctors · Medical Residents · Senior Clinicians · 3 Interactive Demos · 8+ Clinical Templates

₹3,540 incl. GST
Apply Now
A specially designed introductory programme that equips medical professionals with the knowledge and practical awareness to understand how AI is already transforming healthcare. Delivered entirely via video with three interactive demos — no coding knowledge required — serving as the entry point to SAKU's Medical & Healthcare AI pathway.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand in accessible terms what AI is and how it is transforming healthcare
  • Learn why medical data quality matters and how poor data impacts patient outcomes
  • Recognise how AI tools (chatbots, wearables, decision-support) can save doctors' time
  • Build awareness of AI's potential in day-to-day practice without any coding requirement
  • Gain confidence to discuss AI concepts with patients and colleagues

Programme Features

Video Content 100% Video-based
Interactive Demos 3 Demos Included
Clinical Templates 8+ Templates
Entry Requirement No coding needed
Pathway Leads to MD-F & MD-X

Programme Content Headings

01
How AI is Transforming Healthcare — What AI, ML, and DL mean for doctors; global adoption and clinical case examples
02
Why Data Management Matters for Doctors — Medical data as fuel for AI; doctors' role in ensuring data quality
03
How AI Supports Doctors in Daily Practice — Workflow automation (chatbots, EMR); decision support (drug alerts, wearables)
04
Preparing for the Future of AI in Medicine — Skills doctors should acquire; the evolving Doctor-AI partnership

Entry Requirements

MBBS, MD, DNB, or an equivalent medical qualification; no coding or technical background required.
