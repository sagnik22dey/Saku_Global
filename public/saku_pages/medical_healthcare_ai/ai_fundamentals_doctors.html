AI Fundamentals for Doctors

20 Hours · Guided Labs & Demos · Clinical Practitioners / Healthcare Leaders

₹17,700 incl. GST
Apply Now
A hands-on programme moving beyond awareness to provide practical skills in using no-code AI tools for clinical applications. Learners label medical data, build small AI models, interpret clinical AI outputs, and explore case studies covering pneumonia detection, sepsis prediction, and readmission risk — entirely without coding.

Learning Outcomes

  • Gain practical skills in using no-code AI tools in clinical contexts
  • Label medical data for AI readiness (Normal vs Abnormal X-rays)
  • Train and test no-code AI models including image classifiers
  • Interpret outputs from pre-trained AI models (confusion matrices, ROC curves)
  • Apply learnings to day-to-day clinical decision-making

Programme Features

No-Code Labs Real Medical Datasets
AI Model Building Image & Tabular Projects
Pre-trained Models No Coding Required
Clinical Case Studies Pneumonia - Sepsis - Readmission

Programme Content Headings

01
Introduction to AI in Healthcare — Fundamentals of AI, ML, DL; AI in healthcare workflows
02
Data in Medicine — Foundation of AI — Structured vs unstructured data; data cleaning, privacy; data labelling for doctors
03
AI in Daily Clinical Practice — EMR support, triage, monitoring; demo: chatbots & wearable tools
04
Clinical Case Studies — Diagnostics (pneumonia, cancers); prediction (sepsis, readmission); drug discovery decision support
05
Building an AI Model — Problem definition & data preparation; no-code image and tabular model building
06
Ethics, Legal & Practical Concerns — AI bias; patient consent; clinical safety considerations
07
Future of AI in Medicine — Robotics, genomics, personalised care; the Doctor-AI partnership

Entry Requirements

MBBS, MD, DNB, or equivalent; completion of MD-E is recommended but not mandatory.
