Leadership At Saku Global

Dr. Kamtekar brings over 25 years of global leadership to Saku Global Tech Labs, driving innovation in semiconductor education and advanced materials.

He previously served as Chief Academic Officer at MIT World Peace University, where he oversaw 12 schools including Engineering and Management, and held senior executive roles at technology firms such as CAMECA, Moxtek, and XOS.

Dr. Kamtekar holds a Ph.D. in Analytical Instrumentation from the University of Massachusetts Lowell, and an MBA in Managerial Finance and Strategy from the Kellstadt Graduate School of Business, DePaul University - with international experience based in the USA.

Under his leadership, Saku Global develops cutting-edge training programs in semiconductors and AI. In his free time, he mentors aspiring engineers and enjoys exploring the latest tech trends.
